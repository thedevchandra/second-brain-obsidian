# COCHINITA PIBIL - slow-roasted Mexican pork

- Pork shoulder
- 1 1⁄2 cups distilled white vinegar
- 3 cloves garlic
- 2.5 oz Annatto or achiote
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- 1⁄2 tsp ground cumin
- 1⁄2 tsp ground cinnamon
- 2 1⁄2 cups orange juice
- 1⁄4 yellow onion

1. Place all the ingredients for the sauce in a blender.
2. Add the orange juice, vinegar, onion, and spices and blend all the ingredients together until they are very well mixed.
3. Add the sauce from the blender to the Instant Pot.
4. Cook on Manual High Pressure for 60 minutes.
5. Once the cooking time has completed, allow for a 30-minute natural pressure release.
6. Remove the meat from the pot and shred.
7. Pour some of the liquid from the pot over the shredded meat and allow to marinate in the liquid until ready to serve.

# Pickled Red Onion

- 1-2 red onion
- 1 habanero peppers
- 1/2 Lime juice
- 1 cup Orange juice
- Some fresh oregano
- Black pepper
- 1 tbsn Distilled vinegar.

1. Finely chop onion.
2. Add all the ingredients with a little salt and let it rest for about 30-minutes.

# SALSA TATEMADA

- 2 pieces of ripe red tomato
- 5 pieces of tomatillo
- 1 or 2 pieces of serrano pepper, depending on how spicy you like your sauce.
- 1 clove garlic
- 1 piece of white onion

1. Heat the skillet until it is extremely hot.
2. Add all the ingredients at the same time and let them cook for 35-40 minutes.
3. Grind all the ingredients to make a chunky sauce.
4. Add salt to taste.

# RAW GREEN SAUCE

- 5 pieces of tomatillo
- 1/2 of a small or medium avocado
- 1 bunch of coriander with pod
- 1 piece of onion
- 1 or 2 chile de árbol
- Half lemon

1. Mix all the ingredients in a blender.
2. Add lemon and salt to taste and grind completely.
