**Drug and Alcohol Program Advisor (DAPA):**

- Managed by a designated DAPA at the command level.
- Provides education, training, referrals, and support related to drug and alcohol abuse prevention for SELRES in drill status.

**Sexual Assault Prevention and Response (SAPR):**

- Managed by a designated SAPR officer.
- Provides prevention, response, and support services for victims.
- Both NRA and NRAU assist with program management through training, awareness campaigns, support services, and reporting.
- Two reporting options - Restricted Reporting and Unrestricted Reporting.
    - Under **Unrestricted Reporting**, both the command and law enforcement are notified.
    - **Restricted (Confidential) Reporting -** the adult sexual assault victim can access healthcare, advocacy services, and legal services without the notification to command or law enforcement.

**Command Urinalysis Program:**

- Managed by a Urinalysis Program Coordinator (UPC).
- Conducts random or unit sweep urinalysis tests to ensure compliance with drug policies.
- NRAU assists with program management through participation, coordination, and education.

**Command Managed Equal Opportunity (CMEO):**

- Managed by a CMEO Manager.
- CO/OIC of NRAU required to post CMEO statements.
- Command climate surveys assess CMEO effectiveness; NRA and NRAU contribute to improving command climate.

**Family Advocacy Program (FAP):**

- Managed by a FAP Manager.
- Provides support for domestic violence, child abuse, and neglect.
- SELRES in drill status have access to prevention programs, victim advocacy, and referrals.

**Government Travel Charge Card (GTCC):**

- Managed by cardholders, APC, AO, CO/OIC, and DFAS.
- Intended for official government travel expenses only.
- Non-payment can lead to account suspension, cancellation, and disciplinary action.

**Physical Fitness Assessment (PFA) Program:**

- Conducted annually, consists of BCA, aerobic fitness, and muscular strength/endurance tests.
- PFA failures can impact readiness, promotion, and reenlistment.
- PFA results annotated in EVALs/FITREPs; governed by OPNAV and BUPERS instructions.

**Security Management Program:**

- Manages security clearances, investigations, and training.
- Annual training required for personnel with clearances.
- Additional requirements for OCONUS travel; self-reporting criteria for security clearances.
- Classified material storage, labeling, and destruction must comply with NISPOM guidelines.
