**1.    Primary ZIP Codes to Consider**
    •    **78701**: Downtown Austin, ideal for business networking and a lively lifestyle, though more expensive. Known for its vibrant downtown scene.
    •    **78702**: East Austin, recommended for professionals with a good community atmosphere.
    •    **78704**: South Congress/South Lamar area, slightly touristy but with great neighborhoods and community feel.

    **2.    Housing Options**
    •    Corazon Apartments: Recommended for a two-bedroom unit at approximately $2300.

    **3.    Lifestyle & Networking**
    •    Downtown and East Austin are recommended areas for business growth and community building.
    •    Austin provides ample opportunities for networking and professional growth.

    **4.    Comparisons with Other Cities**
    •    Austin vs. San Diego: Austin is favored over San Diego due to lower cost of living and taxes.

    **5.    General Moving Advice**
    •    Austin is recommended over San Diego for building a business and community.
    •    Consider temporary housing options like Airbnb for flexible stays.

    **6.    Community Building**
    •    Emphasis on finding a place where you can build deep relationships and grow your business.

    **7.    Personal Experiences & Recommendations**
    •    Moving to where the action is, like Downtown or East Austin, is suggested for business and personal growth.
    •    Avoid getting too involved in trends like cryptocurrency as a primary income source.

    **8.    Travel & Lifestyle Reflections**
    •    Long-term travel can lead to a desire for a permanent base to build community and business.
