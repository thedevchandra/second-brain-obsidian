- Strategist - value intelligence and big-picture thinking; creative mastermind that is truly the chess master of details
    - Strength: Excellent communication gatherers who become experts in most topics.
    - Hacks: Continually look for mentally stimulating projects to be sustainable.
    - Ideal Schedule: Plan weekly review sessions with yourself or a colleague to track objectives.
- Performer - energetic and skillful in every endeavor; immediate action taker and excel at working under unknown conditions
    - Strength: Creating spontaneous solutions and relationships under difficult circumstances.
    - Hacks: Build in frequent check-ins to remain focused on the most important priorities.
    - Ideal Schedule: Use timed reminders instead of task lists to get things done.
