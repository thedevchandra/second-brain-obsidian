Below is a list of the software and tools that I use to operate my websites. These are listed in no
particular order. Bolded items are software/tools that I can not live without.

1. Ahrefs - For all things SEO (keyword research, competitor analysis, content outreach, link
building, etc) Bonus tip, their YouTube channel has phenomenal content.
2. [hunter.io](http://hunter.io/) - Quickly find email addresses, very useful for when doing outreach
3. HARO - Help A Reporter Out, an easy way to get quoted/guest posts for link building
4. Surfer SEO - An online tool that helps you or your writers structure content so that itâ€™s as SEO
friendly as possible
5. Google Analytics - Track your website traffic and data
6. Google AdSense, MediaVine, AdThrive - Display ad options
7. Google Search Console - Request page indexing by Google crawlers, check for website
issues, and upload new sitemaps
8. MailerLite - Store email subscribers and send out email blasts
9. ODYS Global - A trusted marketplace for buying aged (expired) domains
10. Slack - Communication with hired 3rd parties
11. Click Up - Tracking of tasks, delegation of tasks, and ensuring deadlines are being met
12. ICANN Lookup - Find the age of domains and owner contact info
13. Copyscape - A paid plagiarism checker
14. Rocket CDN - Content delivery network
15. GTmetrix - Website performance testing
16. Google PageSpeed Insights - Website performance testing
17. WordCounter - Counts number of words on a page
