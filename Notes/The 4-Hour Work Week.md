A revolutionary guide to escaping the 9-5 grind and designing your ideal lifestyle. Ferriss introduces the concept of lifestyle design, teaching how to automate income, eliminate time-wasters, outsource tasks, and achieve more by working less. Challenges traditional retirement planning by advocating for "mini-retirements" throughout life.
