## Travel Tips

- Principles
    - People First - Travel Companion Compatibility - Choose trips based on people first and experiences, destinations, and restaurants - Menfluential, LA
    - Presence - Remove the distractions (social media, news, etc.) and be present with your people and environment
    - Creativity - Find the most creative ways to get from Point A to Point B and lodging - taking a train between Seattle and Vancouver to relax and get scenery; staying with an AirBnB host
    - Spontaneity - Don't plan everything and leave room for adventure
    - Purpose - Give the trip purpose or a theme - conference, birthday, or anniversary
- Ways to Experience Cities
    - Walking Tours - many are pay what you think itâ€™s worth
    - Bike Tours - the best way to see the city
    - Boat Cruises - see the city on water
    - Bar Crawls - experience nightlife and meet people
    - Stay with a Friend - get the local perspective
    - Events - give travel a purpose and meet people

## Travel Tools

### Trip Planning

- [Trip It](https://www.tripit.com/web) - organizes your travel plans and creates itineraries for every trip
- [Award Wallet](https://awardwallet.com/) (replace with TPG app) - tracks all loyalty points, miles, and rewards
- [Nomad List](https://nomadlist.com/) - finds the best places in the world to live, work and travel remotely
- [American Express Lounge Collection](https://www.americanexpress.com/idc/en/benefits/the-platinum-card/global-lounge-collection.html) - Centurion, Priority Pass, Delta Sky Club, etc.
- [LoungeBuddy](https://www.loungebuddy.com/) - discover, book, and access premium airport lounges worldwide
- [Remote Year](https://www.remoteyear.com/) - multi-month work and travel program to live in a new country every month
