Website owners quick reference checklist for efficient operation

______ Are all affiliate links and linked pages opening in a new tab?
______ Does the site have an SSL installed (i.e. https vs http)?
______ Have you used Google PageSpeed Insights to check page loading speeds?
______ Is Rocket CDN installed?
______ Is Short Pixel Image Optimizer installed?
______ Are your affiliate links pointing to live pages, not dead product pages?
______ If using Amazon Associates, do you have multiple country tracking enabled?
______ If running display ads, are the ads distracting from more profitable affiliate
offerings?
______ Are you creating or updating at least one piece of content per month?
______ Did you submit the URL to Google Search Console for crawling?
______ Is Google Serach Console enabled?
______ Are new backlinks coming in monthly, either organically or through outreach?
______ Does the content “feel good” on mobile, or was mobile an afterthought?
______ Are you utilizing Call To Action buttons to promote affiliate offerings?
______ Are you sending at least 1 email per month to your email subscribers?
______ Did you check your Google Analytics data this month? Know the behavior
______ Can you sell dedicated banner ad space to increase profitability?
______ Are you setting the alt text on images that are embedded in posts?
______ Delete plugins that are you are no longer using

______ Have you ever done content pruning (i.e. delete/301 redirect content that isn’t rank and
that is stale)?
______ Are you article titles both optimized for SEO and attention grabbing?
______ Did you check your top keywords rankings this month? Are they holding, going up, or
down? Why?
______ Are you keeping track of expenses and earnings? You’ll be happy you did if you ever
want to sell
______ Is it feasible to go by pass your affiliate network and make a deal with the retailer/
manufacture for a better commission?
______ Could you set up a Google Ad campaign to push traffic to some of your profitable pages
where the CPC is low?
______ Could you create an e-book/PDF packet to sell to your email subscribers?
