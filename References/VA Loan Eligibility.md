[VA home loan limits](https://www.va.gov/housing-assistance/home-loans/loan-limits/)

VA home loan limits are the same as the Federal Housing Finance Agency (FHFA) limits for one-unit only. These are called conforming loan limits.

[Check current loan limits on the FHFA website](https://www.fhfa.gov/DataTools/Downloads/Pages/Conforming-Loan-Limit.aspx)

| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
