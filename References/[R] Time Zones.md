In the US, daylight saving time (DT) **starts on the second Sunday in March and ends on the first Sunday in November**, with the time changes taking place at 2:00 a.m. local time.

- Sun, Mar 9, 2025 – Sun, Nov 2, 2025
- Sun, Mar 8, 2026 – Sun, Nov 1, 2026
