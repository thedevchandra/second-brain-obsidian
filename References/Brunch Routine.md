A healthy breakfast should include a balance of protein, healthy fats, fiber, and essential nutrients to provide sustained energy and satiety throughout the morning. Here are some nutritious ingredients you can incorporate into your breakfast:

### **Proteins**

1. **Eggs**: Rich in high-quality protein and essential nutrients.
2. **Lean Meats**: Such as turkey or chicken sausage.
3. **Smoked Salmon**: Rich in protein and omega-3 fatty acids.

### **Healthy Fats**

1. **Avocado**: Packed with healthy monounsaturated fats and fiber.
2. **Nuts and Seeds**: Almonds, chia seeds, flaxseeds, and walnuts.
3. **Nut Butters**: Almond butter, peanut butter, or sunflower seed butter.
4. **Healthy Oils**: Avocado oil, coconut oil, extra virgin olive oil.

### **Fiber and Carbohydrates**

1. **Fruits**: Berries, bananas, apples, and citrus fruits.
2. **Vegetables**: Spinach, kale, tomatoes, and bell peppers.
3. **Starchy Vegetables**: Potatoes, hashbrowns, and sweet potatos.
4. **Whole Grains**: Oatmeal or whole grain bread.

### **Additional Options**

1. **Herbs and Spices**: Cinnamon, turmeric, and ginger can enhance flavor and provide additional health benefits.
2. **Supplements**:
    1. Athletic Greens (AG1)

### **Example Healthy Breakfast Ideas:**

1. **Smoothie Bowl**: A blend of spinach, banana, frozen berries, and protein powder, topped with granola, chia seeds, and sliced fruit.
2. **Avocado Toast with Eggs**: Whole grain toast with mashed avocado, a poached or scrambled egg, and a sprinkle of salt and pepper.
3. **Smoked Salmon and Avocado Toast**: Whole grain toast topped with mashed avocado, smoked salmon, capers, and a squeeze of lemon juice
4. **Veggie Omelet**: An omelet filled with spinach, tomatoes, bell peppers, and a sprinkle of cheese, served with a side of whole grain toast.
5. **Oatmeal with Fresh Berries and Nuts**: A bowl of oatmeal topped with blueberries, strawberries, chia seeds, and a drizzle of almond butter.
6. **Sweet Potato and Black Bean Breakfast Burrito**: A whole grain tortilla filled with scrambled eggs, roasted sweet potatoes, black beans, avocado, and a sprinkle of cheese.

Incorporating these ingredients into your breakfast can help ensure you're starting your day with a balanced and nutritious meal.
